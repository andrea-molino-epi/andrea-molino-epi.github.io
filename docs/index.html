Pelle Tracey

Hello! My name is Pelle Tracey. I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Michigan School of Information, where I am advised by Dr. Patricia Garcia. I am part of the Culturally Responsive Computing and AnthroTech Labs, and am an affiliate researcher with the Center for Ethics, Society, and Computing.

I'm interested in automated decision-making, government algorithms, infrastructures, and the future of work. My current work examines how algorithmic prioritization is impacting homeless services systems, the social workers who work within them, and the unhoused people they serve.

Here is my CV and Google Scholar. You can reach me at ptracey[at]umich[d0t]edu.
