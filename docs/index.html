Pelle Tracey

Hello! My name is Pelle Tracey. I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Michigan School of Information, where I am advised by Patricia Garcia and Ricardo Punzalan. I am part of the Culturally Responsive Computing Lab, the Tech.Culture.Matters research group, and the Center for Ethics, Society, and Computing.

I'm interested in automated decision-making, infrastructures, and the future of work. My current work examines how algorithmic assessment is impacting homeless services systems, the social workers who work within them, and the unhoused people they serve.

Here is my CV and Google Scholar. You can reach me at ptracey[at]umich[d0t]edu.
